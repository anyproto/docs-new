# Collaboration

### What is Collaboration in Anytype?

Anytype lets you share Channels with other people so you can work together in real time. When you invite someone to a Channel, they get access to everything in it — Objects, Types, Properties — all synced and encrypted.

Unlike traditional cloud apps where a company mediates access, sharing in Anytype works peer-to-peer. Your data stays encrypted, and only people you explicitly invite can decrypt and see it.

### Why it matters

Whether you're working with a team, sharing a project with a friend, or organizing something with family, collaboration lets you go beyond solo use. Shared Channels give everyone a live, synced workspace where changes appear in real time.

### How it works

Every Channel has a **Members** menu in its settings. From there, you can generate invite links and manage who has access.

There are three roles:

| Role | Can edit Objects | Can change Channel name/settings | Can manage members |
|------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **Owner** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Editor** | Yes | Can change Channel name | No |
| **Viewer** | No (read-only) | No | No |

The Channel Owner is whoever created the Channel. There can only be one Owner.

### Inviting someone to your Channel

1. Open the Channel you want to share.
2. Go to **Channel Settings > Members**.
3. Click to generate an invite link.
4. Choose the link type:
   - **Editor** — they can join immediately and edit anything in the Channel.
   - **Viewer** — they can join immediately with read-only access.
   - **Request Access** — they request to join, and you choose their access level.
5. Send the link to the person you're inviting.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (58).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Managing members

From the Members menu, you can:
- Change a member's role (Editor ↔ Viewer)
- Remove a member from the Channel (they'll receive a notification)

{% hint style="info" %}
Collaboration works across devices. Changes sync automatically when members are online, and offline changes sync the next time they connect.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
Currently, you can only share entire Channels — not individual Objects. If you want to share just one thing publicly, check out [Publish](web-publishing.md).
{% endhint %}
