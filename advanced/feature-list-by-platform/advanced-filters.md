# Advanced Filters

Build complex filter logic with AND/OR conditions and grouped rules.

Basic filters let you narrow down a query by one condition at a time. Advanced filters go further — you can combine multiple conditions and group them to create precise queries, like finding all tasks that are either high priority or due this week.

### Using Advanced Filters

Add an advanced filter from the filter menu and define your conditions in the dedicated bar.
